# BEYR Energy - Website Requirements

## Technical Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js 14+ (App Router)
- **Styling**: CSS Modules
- **Animations**: Framer Motion
- **Email**: Resend
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics 4 & Meta Pixel
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Forms**: Zod + React Hook Form
- **Deployment**: Vercel (with SSL)

## Core Features
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Fast loading (optimized images)
- SEO optimized structure (H1-H2 hierarchy)
- Meta title & meta description for each page
- WhatsApp floating button
- Click-to-call enabled
- Contact form with email routing
- Google Analytics & Meta Pixel integration ready
- SSL secured
- Basic on-page SEO

## Key Assets Needed
1. **High-resolution hero & product images** — Equipment photos (rental generators, solar panels, inverters, batteries, energy storage units, etc.), team/installation shots.
2. **Content materials** — Text copy for: Home hero & solutions, About Us, Contact details, 5 Solutions pages, 6 Industries pages.
3. **Logo & brand assets** — Official BEYR Energy logo (PNG/SVG), color palette (Dark Navy + Energy Green).
4. **Contact & location info** — Full business address, phone numbers, WhatsApp number, email for form routing.
5. **Analytics / tracking codes** — GA4 property ID, Meta Pixel ID.
